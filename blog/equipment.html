My Equipment

I suppose this is a pretty good place to start when it comes to my work and how I create it. So let us start with the basics: First and foremost; THE CAMERA! I currently use a Nikon D3400. Not the flashiest of cameras, but it gets the job done. I specifically chose this entry level model, due to its bluetooth connectability. This was a must for me! When out and about I do quite a lot of editing on my phone (I’ll let you know what applications I use at a later date) so this feature streamlines the process, and makes keeping the ol’ instagram up to date, easy peasy squeezy lemons :)


Next, Im a big believer in having superb quality lenses. Yeah a high end camera body is always great, but as a part time photographer I’ve found it most prudent to invest in my lenses.


When it comes to using the standard 18-55mm lens, I typically only use this when using my macro/wide angle attachment (a superb find on Amazon). The cheapest ‘lens’ so to speak, that I own and yet it’s been one of the most valuable for creating those up close, intimate nature images.


That’s about it when it comes to heavy investment materials. I do have a couple other must haves up my sleeve as well.
