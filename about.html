About Me

Hi! I’m Phoebe and… welcome, I suppose! Photography has always been my passion and this website is my little space to share that passion with as many people as I can.


I can’t say I really have a style as such- for me, it’s always about that little spark of an idea, that creative thread that grows to the finished project; a project that even I don’t really know what it will look like until it’s done!

But I suppose I do have a few specialities- I love working with models on location shoots and really enjoy portrait work (and take commissions if any creative types need new headshots…. just saying). I also work as an event photographer from time to time.

So if you’re interested in booking a shoot, or just want to chat, please reach out through my social media.

I hope you enjoy my work!
